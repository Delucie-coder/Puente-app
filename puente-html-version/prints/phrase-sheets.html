Puente — 12-week Phrase Sheets (English weeks 1–6, French weeks 7–12)

One page per week. Use for classroom handouts or quick reference.

Week 1 — Greetings & Introductions (English)

Vendor and Moto variants

Vendor phrases (EN)

Hello — Bonjour (FR) (heh-lo)
How can I help you? — Comment puis-je vous aider? (how can I help you)
My name is... — Je m'appelle... (my name is)
Nice to meet you — Enchanté (nice to meet you)
Thank you — Merci (thank you)

Moto phrases (EN)

Where to? — Où allez-vous ? (where to)
Hop on — Montez (hop on)
I’ll take you to... — Je vous emmène à... (I'll take you to)
Wait a moment — Attendez un moment (wait a moment)
See you — À bientôt (see you)

Practice

  1. Pair up and roleplay: vendor greets customer, moto asks destination.
  2. Listen-and-repeat 3 times for each phrase — slow then normal speed.
  3. Quiz: Which phrase asks for a destination?

Week 2 — Numbers & Prices (English)

Vendor phrases (EN)

How much is this? — C'est combien? (how much is this)
It costs [number] — Ça coûte [number] (it costs)
Can you give me a discount? — Pouvez-vous me faire une remise? (discount)

Moto phrases (EN)

The fare is [number] — Le tarif est [number] (fare)
How long? — Combien de temps? (how long)
That will be [amount] — Ça fera [amount] (that will be)

Practice

  1. Number drills: 1–100 aloud.
  2. Mock transactions: vendor practices giving change.

Week 3 — Directions & Places (English)

Vendor phrases (EN)

Where is the market? — Où est le marché? (where is the market)
Turn left / right — Tournez à gauche / droite (turn left/right)

Moto phrases (EN)

Wait by the [landmark] — Attendez près du [repère] (wait near the landmark)
Go straight — Allez tout droit (go straight)

Practice

  1. Map drill: follow spoken directions and point to locations.
  2. Roleplay giving pickup instructions.

Week 4 — Food & Ordering (English)

Vendor phrases (EN)

I would like [item], please — Je voudrais [plat], s'il vous plaît (I would like)
Small / Large — Petit / Grand (small/large)

Moto phrases (EN)

Hold the bag carefully — Faites attention au sac (be careful)
Hot — be careful — Chaud — faites attention (hot)

Practice

  1. Menu-reading practice and ordering roleplay.

Week 5 — Time & Scheduling (English)

Vendor phrases (EN)

We open at 7 am — Nous ouvrons à 7h (we open at)
Come back tomorrow — Revenez demain (come back tomorrow)

Moto phrases (EN)

I will arrive in 10 minutes — J'arrive dans dix minutes (I will arrive)
Wait 5 minutes — Attendez cinq minutes (wait)

Practice

  1. Clock reading and arranging pickup times exercises.

Week 6 — Safety & Emergencies (English)

Common phrases

Help! — À l'aide! (help)
I'm injured — Je suis blessé (I'm injured)
Take me to the hospital — Emmenez-moi à l'hôpital (take me to hospital)

Practice tips

Roleplay emergency calls and quick phrases for bystanders.
